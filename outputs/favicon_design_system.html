📐 Design Guidelines (for future reference)

Font

Outfit — weight 700-800 for favicons

Corner Radius

14px at 64px, 8px at 32px, 4px at 16px (or circles)

Sizes to Generate

16×16 (tab), 32×32 (tab HD), 180×180 (apple-touch), 192×192 + 512×512 (PWA manifest)
